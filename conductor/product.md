# Product Definition

## Initial Concept
Vibe Trading is a modern, event-driven trading platform designed to support algorithmic trading, market data analysis, and machine learning-driven predictions. It leverages a multi-language monorepo architecture to combine high-performance Python microservices with a responsive React frontend.

## Vision
To provide a professional-grade, extensible trading ecosystem that democratizes access to institutional-level tools for algorithmic execution, real-time analytics, and AI-powered market insights, empowering users to make data-driven decisions with confidence.

## Core Features
- **Real-time Dashboard:** A comprehensive UI for monitoring portfolio performance, active orders, and live market data.
- **Documentation Hub:** A centralized, MDX-driven site for architecture, API references, and user guides.
- **Algorithmic Trading Engine:** A robust Python-based backend for executing automated trading strategies.
- **Market Data Ingestion:** High-throughput processing of ticks, klines, and order books via Apache Kafka.
- **Advanced Analytics:** Real-time calculation of technical indicators and performance metrics.
- **ML Integration:** Seamless integration of machine learning models for price prediction and trend analysis.

## AI & Research Capabilities
- **Financial Research Analysis:**
    - Summarization and analysis of major institutional financial reports and industry-specific research.
    - AI-driven digest of industry news and key market events.
    - Analysis and summarization of academic papers in the quantitative finance domain.
- **Smart Stock Selection & Screening:**
    - AI-powered quantitative stock screening and selection based on customizable criteria.
- **Single-Stock AI Analysis:**
    - Comprehensive AI reports covering Fundamentals, Technicals, Market Action, Capital Flow, and Sentiment.
    - Actionable outputs: Market Direction, Entry/Exit Points, Trading Style (e.g., Day/Swing), Momentum Strength, and Position Sizing recommendations.
- **Real-time Monitoring & Alerts:**
    - Watchlist management with AI-generated trading signals based on real-time price action.
- **Strategy Development & Optimization:**
    - **AI Backtesting & Reflection:** Support for testing various AI strategies with a "self-reflection" mechanism for dynamic strategy iteration and improvement.
    - **Visual Performance Tracking:**
        - AI trading metadata and backtest results visualization.
        - Calendar view for daily AI trading P&L.
        - Gantt charts comparing daily predictions vs. actual market moves to visualize deviations.
    - **Decision Transparency:** Daily logs of AI Trader reflections and "Chain of Thought" decision-making processes.

## User Personas
- **Algorithmic Traders:** Users who design and deploy automated trading strategies.
- **Quantitative Analysts:** Researchers leveraging market data for backtesting and model training.
- **Active Traders:** Individuals requiring real-time execution and monitoring tools.
- **Fundamental Investors:** Users seeking AI-synthesized research and deep-dive stock analysis.
