# Task: Design Specs

Figma design interpretation and requirements.

## Figma Design

**Original Request:** "实现这个page供我预览"  
**Design Style:** Modern glassmorphism with finance-focused colors

**Key Specifications:**
- Container: 1200×1200px
- Header: 60px sticky
- Tiles: Glassmorphism with gradient borders
- Colors: 3-zone dynamic system
- Interactions: Hover lift, sparkline reveal

---

## Design Adaptations

**From Figma to Implementation:**
- Dual backgrounds method (border-image workaround)
- Conservative color palette (no decorative colors)
- Chinese market convention (red=up enforced)
- Breathing dots (added attention level indicator)
- Sparkline integration (30-day price history)
